Welcome

Music is beautiful. Instruments are one of the things that help in creating music other than the voice and lyrics in this world. Thus we, Gio, Frances, Franc and Gian were inspired to create this website about the the different families of musical instruments. We want other people to learn more about the families and help them gain more knowledge about music.

There are people out there in the world that have no clue about the differences of each instrument family. Instruments are like humans in terms of traits. All are distinct with unique properties and characteristics. So, we wish to help others be familliarized with them and, in turn, come to appreciate them as well.

We, the creators, hope that you find this website helpful in discovering and learning more about musical instruments. We had a wonderful time creating this site. There were a lot of sleepless night, cups of coffee, and cursing involved in the making of this project.
